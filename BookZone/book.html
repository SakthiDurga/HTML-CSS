Rich dad, Poor dad

Robert Kiyosaki

teaches financial literacy, contrasting his educated but financially struggling "Poor Dad" with his friend's entrepreneurial "Rich Dad," highlighting that the rich make money work for them by acquiring income-generating assets (businesses, real estate, stocks) rather than liabilities (debts, things that cost money like most homes), focusing on financial intelligence to escape the "rat race" of working just for a paycheck
